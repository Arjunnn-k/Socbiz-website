About Winter Analytics

Winter Analytics is the annual flagship competition hosted by the Society of Business, IIT Roorkee. Built exclusively for first-year undergraduates, it serves as an entry point into the world of data-driven problem solving, strategic thinking, and real-world analytics.

Participants engage with complex, real-world-inspired datasets and are expected to derive insights, identify trends, and present well-structured, data-backed recommendations‚Äîsimilar to what an analytics or consulting team would do in practice.

With over 200 freshers participating each year, Winter Analytics has established itself as one of the most competitive and intellectually engaging events for first-year students on campus. The competition is conducted in multiple stages, designed to evaluate participants across a range of analytics and communication capabilities.
